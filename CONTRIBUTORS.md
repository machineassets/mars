## How to Contribute

MARS is an open standard actively seeking contributions from:

- **Robotics engineers** — schema validation, ROS2 integration, field definitions
- **Insurance actuaries** — MRS formula weights, premium tier validation
- **Fleet operators** — benchmark data from real deployments
- **Standards professionals** — alignment with ISO, IEC, IFR frameworks

See [GOVERNANCE.md](./GOVERNANCE.md) for the full contribution process.

To get started: open a [GitHub Discussion](https://github.com/machineassets/mars/discussions) or email [emmanuel@machineassets.com](mailto:emmanuel@machineassets.com).
